Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing to play our games. At Ski Resort Riders, LLC, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

In addition to this notice, we encourage you to carefully review our Terms of Service, which govern your use of our Services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from you when you use our games:

  • Personal information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and other contact information.
  • Device information: We may collect information about your device, including the type of device, operating system, and mobile network information. We do not collect geolocation data, although it may be used on your device, inside the game. We may collect geolocation metadata (is your device in Rusutsu Resort premises versus in Hokkaido versus elsewhere in the world).
  • Gameplay information: We may collect information about your gameplay, such as your scores and achievements.
  • Analytics information: We may collect analytics information about your use of the games, including your interactions with the game and the features you use. We may collect analytics about advertisements you have seen inside the game.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our games: We use your information to provide and improve the games, including developing new features and analyzing usage patterns.
  • To communicate with you: We may use your contact information to send you updates about the games, such as new features and promotions.
  • To personalize your game experience: We may use your information to personalize your experience of the games, such as showing you relevant content and ads.
  • To protect our users and our business: We may use your information to detect and prevent fraud, spam, and other illegal activities.
  • To provide you rewards, special offers: We may analyze your in-game actions and achievements to show you rewards, promotions, or other offers from Rusutsu Resorts that are tailored to your personal user experience. We may share your information with Rusutsu Resorts or their partners, so that they may personalize special offers or administer awards.
  • To locate you on the map: We may point out your location inside 3D maps within games like Rusutsu Riders.

How We Protect Your Information

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We also require our service providers to protect your information.

Rusutsu Services and Advertising

We may use Rusutsu services to help us provide the games, rewards and Rusutsu Resort information, such as chairlift or run state. We may advertise Rusutsu Resort and partner services within the game. We may personalize offers for example based on gameplay, location, or time.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to help us provide the games, such as analytics services and ad networks. These third-party services may collect information about your use of the games, such as your device information and gameplay information. We do not control the collection or use of this information by these third-party services.

Cookies

We may use cookies, which are small text files that are stored on your device when you access and use the game. Cookies help us improve your experience of the game and personalize your usage of the game. You may be able to disable cookies or adjust your cookie preferences through your device or browser settings.

Children's Privacy

Our games are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete the information as soon as possible.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we post changes to this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "last modified" date. We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy to be informed of how we protect your information.
